sends information from the local macroscopic cells of MaMiCo (only inner non-ghost cells of this process) to the macroscopic solver. Since the macroscopic solver can have an arbitrary distribution of cells on the processes, the buffer for receiving the cell data is provided to this function in terms of an array of macroscopic cells including the respective global cell indices. This function basically calls for these purposes sendFromMD2MacroNonBlocking(...) and wait4SendFromMD2Macro(...) in a row.
