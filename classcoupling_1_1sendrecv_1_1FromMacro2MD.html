Detailed Description

template<class MacroscopicCell, unsigned int dim>
class coupling::sendrecv::FromMacro2MD< MacroscopicCell, dim >

SendReceiveBuffer for transfer of quantities from a macroscopic solver to the macroscopic cells. Derived from the class coupling::sendrecv::SendReceiveBuffer.

extends the generic SendReceiveBuffer for transfer of quantities from a macroscopic solver to the macroscopic cells of the coupling tool (incl. ghost cells).

sends information from the local macroscopic cells of a macroscopic solver to the macroscopic cells of MaMico (ghost cells are also allowed). Since the macroscopic solver can have an arbitrary distribution of cells on the processes, the buffer for sending the cell data is provided to this function in terms of an array of macroscopic cells including the respective global cell indices. This function calls sendFromMacro2MDNonBlocking(..) and immediately subsequently wait4SendFromMacro2MD(...). Those two methods may alternatively be used, e.g., for "non-blocking" communication.
