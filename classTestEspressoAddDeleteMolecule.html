Detailed Description

test class to check addition and deletion of molecule in espresso. It derives from the scenario provided in TestEspresso.h, containing 8 particles in 2*2*2 domain. We call the add particle and delete particle methods provided in the interface classes and check the number of particles remaining in our domain. Also this test class check the synchronization of ghosts cell due to addition and deletion of particles
